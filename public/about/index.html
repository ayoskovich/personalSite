Who Am I?

Hello!

  My name is Anthony and you’ve found my corner of the internet. In April 2020 I will be graduating from Grand Valley State University with a Bachelor of Science in Statistics and Economics. I also have a math minor.

  I am currently an Advanced Analytics intern at Steelcase, one of the largest office furniture manufacters on the planet. Here I leverage python, R, SQL, and a suite of Microsoft Azure products to analyze data and build models advancing the firm’s strategic objects. I work closely with business professionals to interpret and share these models, and I have learned an enormous amount about how to be an ambassador for effective analytics.

  There is an art to translating the business pain point into a mathematical model. There is also an art in translating that business pain point into a representation using a programming language. The problem may change, or the deliverable may change formats. I try my best to write modular and testable code that can rapidly be pivoted or used in other ways.

  This site hosts my random thoughts, programmy things, statisticy things, and economics things. I enjoy riding bikes, taking pictures with film cameras, and good office furniture!

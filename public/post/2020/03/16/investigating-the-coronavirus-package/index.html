2020/03/16

  Here is an example table.

Province.State Country.Region Lat Long date cases type
Japan 35.6762 139.6503 2020-01-22 2 confirmed
South Korea 37.5665 126.9780 2020-01-22 1 confirmed
Thailand 13.7563 100.5018 2020-01-22 2 confirmed

  Let’s draw a plot. 

coronavirus %>%
  filter(Country.Region %in% c('Singapore', 'Japan', 'Hong Kong')) %>%
  ggplot() +
    geom_point(aes(x=date, y=cases)) +
    facet_grid(Country.Region ~ .)
